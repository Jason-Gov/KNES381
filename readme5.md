To play this game, use arrow keys. There are three levels with increasing difficulties with more obsticles and the character being slightly faster and bigger. 
To win, you have to get to the crown of all three levels before the time limit of 800 seconds ends. If the 500 seconds are up, you would lose the game and would need to restart the game. 
I have set the game to where touching the wall would trigger a penalty where you would move backwards a few steps opposite of the direction you went 
(if you hit the wall going left, you get moved back to the right, if you hit the wall going down, you get moved back upwards an vis versa).
The main reason I did this and not have the game immediately reset if you touch the black lines is to make the game more playable. 
Sometimes you may touch another black wall and move back more, but other times you are able to continue the game with not as severe of a reprecaution. 
I have placed obsticles in the form of fire where if your sprite touches it, you would be sent back to the start of the game.
Each of the fire has different intervals of when it would be on display and when it would not, with one of the fires moving continuously and blocking off a part of the maze for a short period of time.
Upon the sprite touching different parts of the game (the black wall, the fire, the crown and the red barrier to prevent cheating) an audio would play.
To make the game cheat resistance, I disabled dragging the sprites with the mouse, and made the game have continuous collision enforcement. 
I have also implemented a penalty system with the time limit, and I somewhat set boundaries where I restricted getting to the crown from going around the maze with a jumpscare.
To make this game uniquely my own and to add something extra to my game, I had it so each level would have a different sprite to have a story. 
The sprites used are from Pokemon, where each level, the sprite would evolve and grow to help it escape the maze. 
After each sprite completes it's maze, it would stop moving and would be hidden from view.
The changing sprites, the moving fire as well as the fire turning on and off are what I utilized for "uncommon features". 
I used a total of 5 backdrops, 3 for the mazes utilizing maze generator, 2 found online from google used for the winning and losing backdrop. 
I used a total of 5 images, 3 for the sprites utilized found online using google, 1 for the crown found onling using google, and one for the fire found online using google. 
I used a total of 6 audios, all of them found online using the website "myinstants"
